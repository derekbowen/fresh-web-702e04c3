Most hosts close their listing in fall and lose 4-6 months of income. Here's what the top earners do instead:

  • Heated pool? List it as "heated, year-round" and charge a $20-30/hour premium. Demand drops, but per-booking revenue jumps.
  • Hot tub on property? Make it the headline. Hot tub bookings stay strong all winter.
  • Covered patio or fire pit? List the space as a "backyard rental" for small gatherings, photo shoots, and birthday parties even when the pool isn't in use.
  • Photo / video shoots: photographers pay $75-150/hour to shoot at a nice pool. List a "photo shoot" rate.
  • Holiday parties: heated pool + decorated patio = Friendsgiving and December party bookings.

Even an unheated pool can do photo shoots and party rentals through October and again starting March.
